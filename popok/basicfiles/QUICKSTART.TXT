popok - a webmail to pop3/smtp gateway 
by dsw software house
(http://dsw.gesit.com)


=====================================

--This README refers to popok version 0.9--

QUICK START
==========
Setup a new account in your pop3 mail using this information (ie for plasa.com):
- pop3 
    server: localhost
    port: 110
    email address: yourusername@plasa.com
    username: yourusername@plasa.com
    password: your password
- smtp 
    server: localhost
    port: 25
    email address: yourusername@plasa.com
    authentication: "pop before smtp" (if any)
